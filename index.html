iMathIt construction in progress...

Tutorial mode

It makes use of interactive visuals through which the students can experience a new and fun way of learning the course material.
Through an upward swipe gesture from the bottom of the screen, the user is also able to access more information and important formulas on the topic. WolframAlpha has also been integrated to provide the user with more information on the topic.

Timed Challenges. Speed and understanding to the test

Students can excersize their knowledge and undeterstand of various topics such as trigonometry and algebra

Peek

We have also added a feature that lets user glance through the list of questions in the middle of a challenge. They simply need to slide the current view away using the peek button located on the top left corner of the challenge display. With this, users enjoy the flexibility of being able to jump between different questions in a challenge.
